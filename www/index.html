FAQ's Frequently Asked Questions


We are here to help you 7 days a week and respond within 24 hours.
Plus, you can find most answers to your questions right on this page.
  • What is a hackathon?

    A hackathon is an intensive event where teams of developers, designers, and innovators come together to collaborate and create innovative solutions to real-world challenges within a limited timeframe.

  • Who can participate in Hackathon X 2023?

    Hackathon X is open to all aspiring developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts from any background or skill level. Whether you are a student, professional, or hobbyist, you are welcome to join and showcase your talent.

  • Do I need to have coding experience to participate?

    While coding experience can be beneficial, it is not a requirement. Hackathons are a great opportunity to learn and collaborate with others, so anyone passionate about technology and innovation can participate.

  • How do I form a team for the hackathon?

    You can either form a team with your friends or join as an individual and get matched with other participants during team formation sessions at the event.

  • What should I bring to the hackathon?

    Bring your laptop, chargers, any necessary software, and a lot of enthusiasm! Additionally, don't forget to bring your ID for registration purposes.

  • What is the duration of the hackathon?

    Hackathon X is a 24-hour event, giving you ample time to work on your projects and bring your ideas to life.

  • What happens after the hackathon ends?

    After the hackathon concludes, you'll have the opportunity to showcase your project to a panel of judges and fellow participants. Prizes will be awarded to the most innovative and impactful projects.

  • How can I register for Hackathon X 2023?

    You can register for Hackathon X on our website [insert website link]. Hurry, as spots are limited!
